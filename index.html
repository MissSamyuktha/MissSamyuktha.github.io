01. About

I'm a GenAI Data Scientist at GlobalData in Hyderabad, where I design and ship LLM-powered systems end to end: crawling and structuring unstructured web data, building knowledge graphs, and orchestrating multi-agent pipelines that non-technical analysts use every day.

My path here wasn't the usual one: a Mechanical Engineering degree from NIT Kurukshetra, twice qualifying for the Civil Services (IAS/IPS) Mains, one of India's most competitive exams, and then a deliberate pivot into AI through a Postgraduate Program in AI & Machine Learning at the University of Texas, Austin. That mix taught me structured reasoning under pressure and how to learn hard things fast.

What I care about: systems that actually make it to production. Evidence-backed outputs, validation layers, guardrails, and interfaces that real stakeholders can use, not just notebooks.
